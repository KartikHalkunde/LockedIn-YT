Features

LockedIn gives you complete control over your YouTube experience with 11 powerful, customizable toggles. Each feature can be enabled or disabled with a single click from the extension popup, allowing you to create a personalized, distraction-free browsing environment. All features work entirely on your device with zero data collection - your privacy is never compromised. Whether you want to hide the endless feed, block Shorts, remove comments, or disable autoplay, LockedIn puts you in the driver's seat. Browse YouTube intentionally, watch what you came for, and take back control from the algorithm.

Homepage Feed Control

Take back your homepage. LockedIn removes the endless feed of recommended videos, giving you a clean space. No more getting sucked into the algorithm before you even start.

  • ✓ Hide Homepage Feed - Clean, distraction-free homepage
  • ✓ Hide Shorts - Block endless scrolling
  • ✓ Hide Sidebar - Remove trending distractions

Video Page Control

Watch what you came for, nothing more. When you're on a video page, LockedIn removes all the elements designed to keep you clicking. Focus on the content you chose, then move on with your day.

  • ✓ Hide Live Chat - Focus on the content
  • ✓ Hide End Cards - No autoplay suggestions
  • ✓ Hide Comments - Avoid rabbit holes
  • ✓ Disable Autoplay - Watch intentionally

Search Results Control

Find what you're looking for without the noise. Clean search results mean you get exactly what you searched for, without YouTube trying to redirect your attention to trending content or shorts.

  • ✓ Hide Search Recommendations - Clean results only
  • ✓ Hide Shorts in Search - Remove from results
  • ✓ Hide Explore - Remove distractions
  • ✓ Hide "More From YouTube" - Clean sidebar
